Hakeem Condotti

Director

Hakeem began his career at Sony Centre, United Kingdom as a project Manager where he developed and implemented business growth strategies. He returned to Nigeria in 2005 and joined MTech Limited as Head of Product Development and later General Manager, East Africa where he was tasked with the responsibility of establishing the company’s operations in Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania. He later joined Optima Media group in 2010 to manage the company’s operations in Kenya and Ghana.

Hakeem co-founded Terragon Limited and Etra Oilfield Services Nigeria and is presently an Executive Director at MComm Solutions Services, a mobile technology solutions company. Hakeem is an experienced Management professional with vast experience in Project Implementation, Technology solutions and Upstream Oil & Gas servicing. He holds a B.Sc. degree in Economics & Statistics from the University of Benin and an M.Sc. in International Business Economics from the University Of Westminster, UK.
